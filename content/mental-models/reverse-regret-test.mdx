The reverse regret test asks a simple question: which option would I be more at peace with having chosen, even if it failed?

It moves the decision away from short-term fear and toward long-term self-respect.

## Invite your future self

Imagine explaining the choice five years from now. Not the outcome. The choice.

Would you respect the reasoning? Would you understand the courage? Would you forgive the risk?

> Some decisions are not about certainty. They are about becoming someone you can trust.
