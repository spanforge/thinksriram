## The verse

::verse
Bhaja Govindam Bhaja Govindam,
Govindam Bhaja Moodhamate
Naamasmaranat Kimanyat,
Mokshasyaanaadiraahitam
::

## Transliteration

Bhaja govindam bhaja govindam / Govindam bhaja moodhamate / Naama-smarat kimanyat / Mokshasyaanaadir-aahitam

## Translation

Seek Govinda, seek Govinda, seek Govinda, O fool! What else can save you? The path to liberation has always been through the remembrance of the divine name.

## The teaching

The refrain returns again. But by now, its meaning has deepened through all that came before. "Govinda" is no longer just a name of Krishna — it is a pointer to the ground of being, to what is real and enduring in an impermanent world. The fool who is addressed is each of us, in our moments of forgetting.

## Living this today

In your most distracted, anxious, overwhelmed moment — when the demands are crushing, the news is bleak, grief or uncertainty is paralysing — what do you return to? What is your Govinda? It does not have to be a deity. It can be a value: kindness. A practice: breath. A person: your child's face, a parent's voice. A question: what actually matters? Find your anchor and return to it. Not once, but again and again. The practice is the return, not the perfection of the staying. In the moment you forget yourself, the practice is simply to remember — again.
