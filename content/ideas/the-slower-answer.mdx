The fastest answer is sometimes only the loudest one.

Speed can be useful. But not every question is improved by pressure.

## Let the question breathe

When you give a question time, weaker answers fall away. The real shape appears.

> Patience is not delay when it protects the quality of attention.

Some insights do not need to be chased. They need to be welcomed.
