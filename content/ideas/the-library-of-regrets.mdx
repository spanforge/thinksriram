Every life contains a library of unwritten pages.

Some are merciful. Not every door should be opened. But some regrets keep returning because they are not asking for nostalgia. They are asking for courage.

## The signal inside regret

Regret can become a teacher when we stop using it as a punishment.

It points to values we ignored, conversations we delayed, and risks that still matter.

> The question is not what you regret. The question is what the regret is still trying to protect.
