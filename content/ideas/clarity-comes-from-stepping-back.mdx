Problems feel larger when we stand too close to them.

Up close, every detail becomes urgent. Every emotion becomes evidence. Every small movement feels like a verdict.

## The wider frame

Stepping back is not avoidance. It is a way of giving reality more room to speak.

You notice what changed. You notice what did not. You notice the pattern that was invisible from inside the noise.

> A wider view often turns panic into proportion.

The next time a problem feels total, do not rush to solve it from the center. Walk to the edge. Look again.
